About AVtory

  • AVtory is a web application that has helps manage equipment and inventory.
  • Purpose:
    • Improve efficiency by providing employees with a secure and user-friendly interface to interact with their equipment stored in a database.
  • Problem Statement:
    • Many organizations manage their inventory through custom spreadsheets or text documents, which are error prone and time consuming.
  • Alternative Solutions:
    1. Commercial solutions to this problem exist, but they are expensive.
    2. Open source solutions also exist, but most of them lack essential features, such as the ability to check out equipment.
  • Therefore, we decided to create our own system to manage inventory.
